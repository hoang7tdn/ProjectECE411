Bluetooth Smartpen
=============
My Bluetooth Smartpen is a pen that could connect to your smart device (PC, laptop, smartphone or tablet …) by bluetooth wave  . BS could stores everything when you use it for drawing or writing electronically.

Wacom is the product has the same function as BS. But Wacom has two devices, that includes pen and tablet. So that BS is very convenient for users because it is small and can be used for any of your smart devices. And the important things is that its costs is cheaper than Wacom because it just has one device (instead of two) .
